📖 About the Journal

The Journal of Materials and Structures (JMS) is a national, peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to advancing knowledge in materials science and structural engineering. Published since 2020, JMS offers a platform for researchers, engineers, and scientists to share original research, review articles, and technical notes. All submissions undergo a rigorous double-blind peer review process to ensure the highest scientific quality and integrity.

📢 Call for Papers

The journal welcomes contributions in: advanced materials, composite materials, nanomaterials, structural analysis, finite element modeling, construction materials, smart materials, and sustainable engineering solutions.

Clear rules for preparing, formatting and submitting a scientific manuscript.

📝 Manuscript preparation

  • The manuscript must be original and unpublished
  • The abstract should clearly state objectives, methods, results and conclusions
  • Figures and tables must be numbered and cited in the text
  • References must be complete and consistent
  • Ethics, plagiarism and authorship responsibility are required
